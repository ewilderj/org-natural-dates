* org-natural-dates

Natural language date parsing for Org mode scheduling, deadlines, and capture.

Type what you mean — =tomorrow at 3pm=, =every Friday=, =in 3 days= — and let Org figure out the timestamp.

** Examples

| You type                              | Org result                               |
|---------------------------------------+------------------------------------------|
| =tomorrow=                            | =SCHEDULED: <2026-02-22 Sun>=            |
| =next Tuesday at 10am=                | =SCHEDULED: <2026-02-24 Tue 10:00>=      |
| =in 3 days=                           | =SCHEDULED: <2026-02-24 Tue>=            |
| =every Friday=                        | =SCHEDULED: <2026-02-27 Fri +1w>=        |
| =every 2 weeks=                       | =SCHEDULED: <2026-02-21 Sat +2w>=        |
| =every day at 5pm=                    | =SCHEDULED: <2026-02-21 Sat 17:00 +1d>=  |
| =due tomorrow= (in capture)           | =DEADLINE: <2026-02-22 Sun>=             |

Works with =org-schedule=, =org-deadline=, Org Agenda, and =org-capture=.

** Installation

*** MELPA (coming soon)

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
M-x package-install RET org-natural-dates RET
#+end_src

*** use-package with straight.el

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(use-package org-natural-dates
  :straight (org-natural-dates :type git :host github :repo "ewilderj/org-natural-dates")
  :after org
  :bind (:map org-mode-map
         ("C-c C-s" . org-natural-dates-schedule)
         ("C-c C-d" . org-natural-dates-deadline)
         :map org-agenda-mode-map
         ("C-c C-s" . org-natural-dates-agenda-schedule)
         ("C-c C-d" . org-natural-dates-agenda-deadline))
  :config
  (add-hook 'org-capture-before-finalize-hook #'org-natural-dates-capture-finalize-hook))
#+end_src

*** Manual

Clone and add to your =load-path=:

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(add-to-list 'load-path "/path/to/org-natural-dates")
(require 'org-natural-dates)
#+end_src

** Configuration

Bind the commands over the standard Org scheduling keys:

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
;; In org-mode buffers
(with-eval-after-load 'org
  (define-key org-mode-map (kbd "C-c C-s") #'org-natural-dates-schedule)
  (define-key org-mode-map (kbd "C-c C-d") #'org-natural-dates-deadline))

;; In the Org Agenda
(with-eval-after-load 'org-agenda
  (define-key org-agenda-mode-map (kbd "C-c C-s") #'org-natural-dates-agenda-schedule)
  (define-key org-agenda-mode-map (kbd "C-c C-d") #'org-natural-dates-agenda-deadline))
#+end_src

With a prefix argument (=C-u C-c C-s=), the commands fall back to standard =org-schedule= / =org-deadline= behaviour (calendar picker, date removal, etc.).

** Supported input formats

- *Relative dates:* =tomorrow=, =today=, =yesterday=, =in 3 days=
- *Named days:* =Monday=, =next Tuesday=, =last Wednesday=, =Fri=
- *Times:* =at 5pm=, =at 19:00=, =@ 9=
- *Recurrences:* =every day=, =every 2 weeks=, =every Friday=, =every other month=
- *Ordinals:* =on the 15th of every month=, =on the 2nd of every month=
- *Combined:* =every Monday at 10am=, =tomorrow at 3pm=
- *Deadline keywords:* =due Friday=, =by tomorrow=, =deadline in 3 days=

** Org Capture integration

Add the finalize hook to parse natural dates from capture headlines automatically:

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(add-hook 'org-capture-before-finalize-hook #'org-natural-dates-capture-finalize-hook)
#+end_src

With this, typing =* TODO Brush teeth every day at 5pm= during capture (=C-c c=) will, on finalize (=C-c C-c=), clean the headline to =* TODO Brush teeth= and insert the correct =SCHEDULED:= timestamp.

Deadlines are auto-detected via keywords: =due Friday=, =deadline in 3 days=, =by tomorrow=.
